About Us

Who We Are

We are a passionate team of students and technologists committed to exploring and sharing the current and future impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in India. Our project focuses on presenting both global and regional trends in AI to spread awareness and inspire innovation.

Project Goal

This initiative was created as part of our course project, with the aim to:

  • Educate the public on emerging AI technologies
  • Highlight India’s contributions to AI research and development
  • Encourage curiosity through interactive content like videos, visuals, and quizzes

Our Mission

We believe AI can empower communities, improve public services, and fuel entrepreneurship. By showcasing AI trends in India, we aim to foster interest in ethical, inclusive, and impactful AI solutions.
