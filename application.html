Recent AI-Powered Innovations

1. Jugalbandi (Chatbot for Government Schemes)

Developed under Microsoft Research India, Jugalbandi is a chatbot that uses generative AI and WhatsApp to help citizens understand government schemes in their own language.

2. Kisan AI

This AI-based mobile application provides Indian farmers with real-time insights on crop disease detection, weather predictions, and best sowing practices using image recognition and satellite data.

3. AI-Powered CT Scan Analyzer

Startups like Qure.ai have developed AI models that assist radiologists in detecting anomalies like COVID-19, brain hemorrhages, and tuberculosis from scans with high accuracy.

4. MyGov Helpdesk (ChatGPT Integration)

The Indian government has integrated ChatGPT with the MyGov WhatsApp bot, allowing citizens to ask questions and get responses in natural language, enhancing e-governance.

5. Personalized Learning Tools

EdTech companies like BYJU'S and Embibe are leveraging AI to personalize learning paths for students by analyzing their performance data and adapting content delivery accordingly.
