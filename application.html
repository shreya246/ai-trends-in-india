Recent AI-Powered Innovations

1. Jugalbandi – Chatbot for Government Schemes

Created by Microsoft Research India, this AI chatbot helps citizens understand government schemes in their native language via WhatsApp and generative AI.

2. Kisan AI – Smart Farming Assistant

A mobile app that offers Indian farmers AI-powered crop disease detection, weather forecasting, and image-based analysis to improve yield.

3. AI-Driven CT Scan Analysis

Companies like Qure.ai assist doctors in detecting diseases such as tuberculosis and COVID-19 through advanced AI-based image analysis.

4. MyGov Helpdesk + ChatGPT

The Indian government integrated ChatGPT into the MyGov WhatsApp bot, allowing citizens to ask questions in natural language for better digital governance.

5. Personalized Learning Platforms

EdTech firms like BYJU’S and Embibe use AI to customize learning paths for students by analyzing performance and adapting content in real-time.
